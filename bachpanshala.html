InAmigos Education Initiative

Project Bachpanshala

Project Bachpanshala is InAmigos Foundation's signature primary education initiative. It is designed to act as a bridge for children who have either dropped out of school due to economic hardship or lack access to standard educational infrastructure. By setting up neighborhood-level learning centers, we teach, mentor, and shape young lives to build bright careers.

Core Objectives

Education is the primary catalyst that breaks generational cycles of poverty. Project Bachpanshala is committed to giving children from low-income clusters a supportive, vibrant, and interactive space to read, write, play, and grow.

📝 Bridge Education Programs

Offering basic primary school curriculum (Alphabets, Math, Science) and remedial classes for kids behind in school.

💻 Digital Literacy Basics

Introducing children to standard computers, mobile apps, digital tools, and basic safe browsing to prepare them for a modern era.

💡 Life Skills & Hygiene

Conducting workshops on self-confidence, soft skills, dental hygiene, clean hands, and environmental awareness.

🎒 Learning Kits & Stationeries

Distributing books, notebooks, pens, schoolbags, and geometry items to ensure no child sits in a classroom empty-handed.

How Interns and Volunteers Contribute

Youth mentors can directly influence a child's worldview and future aspirations. Here is how you can support this initiative:

  • Act as a part-time educator or guest speaker at a local Bachpanshala center.
  • Help prepare simplified, interactive study materials and lesson plans.
  • Conduct digital skills workshops or basic language speech training.
  • Coordinate local book donation drives to build tiny reading corners.
