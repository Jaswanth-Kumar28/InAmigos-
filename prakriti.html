InAmigos Green Campaign

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti represents InAmigos Foundation's environmental conservation, climate action, and green campaign division. Focused on restoring biodiversity and combating global warming, Project Prakriti plans and implements micro-level ecological cleanups and massive afforestation programs. We strive to create sustainable environmental practices directly inside local residential areas.

Core Objectives

Healthy ecosystems are critical for human wellbeing, fresh breathing air, and agricultural security. Project Prakriti works aggressively on structural tree planting and eco-friendly awareness campaigns to create a cleaner, greener planet.

🌳 Tree Plantation Drives

Planting native tree saplings (Neem, Banyan, Peepal) across deforested patches, public parks, and highway roadsides regularly.

🧹 Public Sanitation Cleanup

Organizing mass plastic collection and garbage cleanups around riverbeds, street corners, public parks, and market places.

♻️ Saying No to Single-Use Plastic

Distributing free biodegradable cotton and jute carry-bags to shopkeepers and local buyers to decrease plastic usage.

🌱 Organic & Green Habits

Educating community clusters on organic home gardening, kitchen waste composting, and smart rainwater harvesting setups.

How Interns and Volunteers Contribute

Climate action begins with small, hands-on physical changes inside our local environments:

  • Coordinate localized tree planting campaigns in your housing community or school campus.
  • Organize weekly zero-plastic cleanup groups and waste collection drives.
  • Run digital and offline awareness campaigns on environmental preservation.
  • Help prepare seed bombs for rapid greening of open, empty land patches.
