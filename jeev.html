InAmigos Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Project Jeev is InAmigos Foundation's specialized animal welfare and rescue wing. Built on the premise of coexistence and compassion, our mission is to ensure that stray animals are fed, sheltered from harsh climates, treated for injuries, and protected from abuse. Through community awareness and street-level actions, Project Jeev acts as a voice for the voiceless.

Core Objectives

Street animals, primarily stray dogs, cats, and cows, face severe dehydration, malnutrition, and road traffic accident risks daily. Project Jeev stands as a continuous community protection system to give these creatures a safe and comfortable environment.

🍖 Daily Feeding Initiatives

Deploying teams to feed street dogs, stray cattle, and birds with clean water and fresh, nutritious, pet-friendly food daily.

🏥 Rescue & Medical Relief

Collaborating with local veterinarians to treat injured stray animals, perform dressing on open wounds, and assist in critical cases.

📦 Makeshift Shelters

Constructing simple water-resistant shelters and placing soft gunny-sack bedding to guard stray dogs from freezing temperatures and heavy rains.

🎗️ Reflective Collar Drives

Placing custom reflective neon collars on stray animals to enhance their visibility to fast-moving vehicles at night and prevent accidents.

How Interns and Volunteers Contribute

Animal welfare requires dedicated street action and soft, caring hearts. Here is how you can support Project Jeev:

  • Lead local feeding drives in your neighborhood using custom food schedules.
  • Coordinate with animal hospitals to report, transport, or treat injured stray dogs.
  • Initiate water-bowl distribution campaigns by placing clay bowls outside houses during summers.
  • Promote stray animal adoption campaigns instead of purchasing breeds.
