InAmigos Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Project Udaan represents InAmigos Foundation's commitment to gender equality and women's self-reliance. Guided by the motto "Give a woman wings to fly," this initiative focuses on offering vocational skills training, hygiene awareness, and enterprise support to rural and semi-urban women. By fostering financial independence, Project Udaan empowers women to make confident choices.

Core Objectives

Empowering a woman triggers a ripple effect that positively impacts her family, her neighborhood, and the next generation. Project Udaan acts as a strong training and advocacy catalyst to build women's confidence and self-sufficiency.

🧵 Vocational Sewing & Designing

Conducting training courses in tailoring, embroidery, traditional handicrafts, and bag-making to help women set up micro-enterprises.

💰 Financial Literacy Training

Educating women on basic banking practices, digital payments, government loan schemes, and managing micro-savings groups.

🩸 Sanitary & Health Hygiene

Driving menstrual health awareness camps in rural pockets, breaking stigmas, and distributing free biodegradable sanitary pads.

📣 Gender Rights & Protection

Hosting interactive legal literacy talks, workshops against domestic abuse, and counseling panels led by professional counselors.

How Interns and Volunteers Contribute

Supporting women requires collaborative community organizing and specialized skill training:

  • Design basic digital marketing plans to help rural women sell their handmade crafts.
  • Lead fundamental financial bookkeeping or digital tools workshops.
  • Coordinate menstrual health awareness sessions and sanitary collection drives.
  • Help compile educational infographics on fundamental rights and self-help schemes.
