InAmigos Youth Development

Project Vikas

Project Vikas is the primary youth skill building, internship, and professional development engine of InAmigos Foundation. Focused on bridging the gap between collegiate education and actual industry needs, this division has trained and supported over 30,000 interns nationwide. We supply training inside digital, administrative, and creative roles to build future-ready professionals.

Core Objectives

Real-world experience is the single greatest asset for a launching career. Project Vikas gives students and fresh graduates a robust corporate structure to practice skills, learn from senior leaders, and lead impactful teams.

📈 Business & Digital Marketing

Providing hands-on practice in SEO optimization, graphic design, content writing, copywriting, and social media brand growth.

👩‍💻 HR Operations & Management

Giving students experience in corporate recruiting, team management, scheduling, talent acquisition, and basic leadership dynamics.

💰 Corporate Finance & Fundraising

Training in budget planning, financial calculations, corporate social responsibility (CSR) strategy, and donor relationship building.

📊 Research & Social Data Operations

Analyzing ground-level welfare data, generating reports on campaign metrics, and carrying out deep target demographic reviews.

How Interns and Volunteers Contribute

Project Vikas is our primary recruitment gateway for corporate talent. When joining our internship family, you will:

  • Work directly on ongoing live campaigns under industry leaders' mentorship.
  • Participate in structured, interactive weekly skill workshops and Q&A sessions.
  • Lead project departments and gain real leadership, management, and collaborative skills.
  • Earn recognized corporate work certifications, recommendation letters, and career references.
